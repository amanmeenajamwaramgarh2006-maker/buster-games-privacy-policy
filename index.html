Privacy Policy for Buster Games Web App

Effective date: December 25, 2025

Buster Games is a web app that offers multiple casual mini-games for entertainment. The app is simple, fast, and easy to use, with no login or registration required.

Buster Games Web App does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data. All gameplay runs locally within the user’s browser.

No user data is collected, transmitted, or stored. The app does not communicate with any backend servers for user data processing.

The app does not use or integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, SDKs, or advertising networks.

5. Security

Since no personal information is collected or stored, there is no risk of user data exposure. All gameplay remains private within the browser session.

This app is suitable for all age groups. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.
